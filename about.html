Born and raised on Long Island, I am a senior at New York University studying history. I’m currently an Editor-at-Large for NYU’s student-run newspaper, Washington Square News. Previously, I served as the Sports Editor and Deputy Managing Editor. At NYU, I have written about university athletics, started my own sports column and contributed to WNYU’s weekly soccer radio show, “The Other Football.”

I also cover women’s basketball for High Post Hoops and SLAM Magazine and write about the NBA and other sports for Uproxx. Last summer, I interned for the National Basketball Association and I spent the fall of 2019 as an intern for SLAM, where I helped run the WSLAM social media accounts.

When I’m not reporting on sports, I can be found doing New York Times crossword puzzles, playing FIFA and reading coming-of-age novels.

You can find me on Twitter at @belakirpalani.
